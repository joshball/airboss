METAR and SPECI (ASOS, AWOS, ATIS)T, G, VFog (including mist and haze) information is reported in METAR/SPECI from manual and ASOS stations and included in ASOS and ATIS broadcasts. Fog/mist/haze is not included in METARs/SPECIs from most AWOS, nor most AWOS broadcasts, depending on the type of AWOS. FG is reported when visibility is less than 5/8 SM. FZFG is reported when temperature is below 00 °C. BR or HZ is reported for visibilities from 5/8 SM to less than 7 SM, depending on the difference between the temperature and dewpoint. If the difference is 40 °F (~20 °C) or less, then FG is reported. Otherwise, HZ is reported.
