FeatureSymbolDefinition
LowA minimum of atmospheric pressure in two dimensions (closed isobars) on a surface chart, or a minimum of height (closed contours) on a constant-pressure chart. Also known as a cyclone.
HighA maximum of atmospheric pressure in two dimensions (closed isobars) on a surface chart, or a maximum of height (closed contours) on a constant-pressure chart. Also known as an anticyclone.
TroughAn elongated area of relatively low atmospheric pressure or height.
RidgeAn elongated area of relatively high atmospheric pressure or height. May also be used as reference to other meteorological quantities, such as temperature and dewpoint.
