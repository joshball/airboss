The FAA deliversflight services to pilots in the CONUS, Alaska, Hawaii, and Puerto Rico. For pilots in
the CONUS, services are available by phone at 1-800-WX-BRIEF (1-800-992-7433). For pilots in Alaska
call 1-833-AK-BRIEF (1-833-252-7433). Services include, but are not limited to, pre-flight weather
briefings, flight planning, and in-flight advisory services.
