TermDescription
Any fog (visibility less than 5/8 SM) consisting predominantly of water droplets at temperatures less than or equal to 32 °F (0°C), whether or not rime ice is expected to be deposited. FZBR is not a valid significant weather combination and will not be used in TAFs.
Freezing Fog
(FZFG)
Shallow FogThe visibility at 6 ft AGL is greater than or equal to 5/8 SM and the apparent visibility in the fog layer is less than 5/8 SM.
(MIFG)
Fog patches covering part of the airport. The apparent visibility in the fog patch or bank is less than 5/8 SM, with the foggy patches extending to at least 6 ft AGL.
Patchy Fog
(BCFG)
A substantial part of the airport is expected to be covered by fog while the remainder is expected to be clear of fog (e.g., a fog bank). Note: MIFG, PRFG, and BCFG may be forecast with prevailing visibility of P6SM.
