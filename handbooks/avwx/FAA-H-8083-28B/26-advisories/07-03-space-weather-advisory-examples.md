26.7.3 Space Weather Advisory Examples
26.7.3.1 Example Space Weather Advisory—GNSS
Note: “GNSS” is the acronym for Global Navigation Satellite System, which is the term for all the world’s
navigation satellites, which includes the United States’ Global Position Satellites.
