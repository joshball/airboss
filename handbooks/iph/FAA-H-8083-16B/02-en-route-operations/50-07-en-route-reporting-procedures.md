# En Route Reporting Procedures

En Route Reporting Procedures
In addition to acknowledging a handoff to another Center
en route controller, there are reports that should be made
without a specific request from ATC. Certain reports should
be made at all times regardless of whether a flight is in radar
contact with ATC, while others are necessary only if radar
contact has been lost or terminated. [Figure 2-68]
