# Minimum IFR Altitude (MIA)

Minimum IFR Altitude (MIA)
The MIA for operations is prescribed in 14 CFR Part 91. These
MIAs are published on aeronautical charts and prescribed
in 14 CFR Part 95 for airways and routes, and in
14 CFR Part 97 for standard instrument approach
procedures. If no applicable minimum altitude is prescribed
