# Substitute Airway or Route Structures

Substitute Airway or Route Structures
ARTCCs are responsible for specifying essential substitute
airway or route segments (sub-routes) and fixes for use
during scheduled or unscheduled VOR/VORTAC shutdowns.
Scheduled shutdowns of navigational facilities require
planning and coordination to ensure an uninterrupted
flow of air traffic. Aeronautical Information Services, in
coordination with the ARTCCs, determine when the length
of outages or other factors require publication of subroutes and Flight Program Operations (AJW-3) provides
flight inspection services, obstacle clearance verification,
certification, and final approval of substitute routes.
