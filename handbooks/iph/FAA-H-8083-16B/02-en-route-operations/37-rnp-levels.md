# RNP Levels

RNP Levels
An RNP level or type is applicable to a selected airspace,
route, or procedure. As defined in the Pilot/Controller
Glossary, the RNP level or type is a value typically expressed
as a distance in nautical miles from the intended centerline
of a procedure, route, or path. RNP applications also
account for potential errors at some multiple of RNP level
(e.g., twice the RNP level).
