# Takeoff Minimums for Commercial Operators

Takeoff Minimums for Commercial Operators
While Part 121 and Part 135 operators are the primary users
of takeoff minimums, they may be able to use alternative
takeoff minimums based on their individual OpSpecs.
Through these OpSpecs, operators are authorized to depart
with lower-than-standard minimums provided they have
the necessary equipment and crew training.
