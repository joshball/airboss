# Combined Vision System Technology

Combined Vision System Technology
The FAA’s NextGen program will transform the NAS to
accommodate a projected three-fold increase in air
operations in the coming decade. Technological and
systemic changes are being developed to significantly
increase the capacity, safety, efficiency, and security of air
operations in the NAS. The FAA will continue to evaluate,
standardize and regulate emerging and enhanced
technologies to ensure their safe and advantageous use
in the NAS. One key capability envisioned to achieve these
goals is the concept of equivalent visual operations (EVO),
where flight operations continue irrespective of the actual
weather conditions. One way EVO might be attained is by
using a combined vision system (CVS) which combines
real-time EFVS imagery with a database-derived synthetic
rendering of surrounding terrain, obstacles, and flight
environment, to provide a virtual visual flight depiction
for the pilot.
