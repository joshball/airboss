# Reversal of Motion

Reversal of Motion
This illusion can be demonstrated in any of the three planes of
motion. While straight and level, with the pilot’s eyes closed,
the instructor pilot smoothly and positively rolls the aircraft
to approximately a 45° bank attitude. This creates the illusion
of a strong sense of rotation in the opposite direction. After
this illusion is noted, the pilot should open his or her eyes
and observe that the aircraft is in a banked attitude.
