# Conducting an IFR Flight

Conducting an IFR Flight
To illustrate some of the concepts introduced in this chapter,
follow along on a typical IFR flight from the Birmingham
International Airport (BHM), Birmingham, Alabama to
Gulfport-Biloxi International Airport (GPT), Gulfport,
Mississippi. [Figure 10-18] For this trip, a Cessna 182 with
a call sign of N1230A is flown. The aircraft is equipped with
dual navigation and communication radios, a transponder,
and a GPS system approved for IFR en route, terminal, and
approach operations.
