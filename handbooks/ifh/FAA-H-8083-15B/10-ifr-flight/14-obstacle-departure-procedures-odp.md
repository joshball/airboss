# Obstacle Departure Procedures (ODP)

Obstacle Departure Procedures (ODP)
ODPs provide obstruction clearance via the least onerous
route from the terminal area to the appropriate en route
structure. ODPs are recommended for obstruction clearance
and may be flown without ATC clearance unless an
alternate departure procedure (SID or radar vector) has
been specifically assigned by ATC. Graphic ODPs have
(OBSTACLE) printed in the procedure title (e.g., GEYSR
THREE DEPARTURE (OBSTACLE),  CROWN ONE
DEPARTURE (RNAV)(OBSTACLE)).
