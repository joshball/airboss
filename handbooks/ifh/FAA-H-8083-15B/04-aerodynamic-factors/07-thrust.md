# Thrust

Thrust
Figure 4-3. The four forces and three axes of rotation.
Weight
Weight is the force exerted by an aircraft from the pull of
gravity. It acts on an aircraft through its center of gravity (CG)
and is straight down. This should not be confused with the
center of lift, which can be significantly different from the
CG. As an aircraft is descending, weight is greater than lift.
Thrust
Thrust is the forward force produced by the powerplant/
propeller or rotor. It opposes or overcomes the force of drag.
As a general rule, it acts parallel to the longitudinal axis.
