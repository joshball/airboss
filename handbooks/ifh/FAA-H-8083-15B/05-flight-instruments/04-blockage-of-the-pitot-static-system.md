# Blockage of the Pitot-Static System

Blockage of the Pitot-Static System
Errors in the ASI and VSI almost always indicate a blockage
of the pitot tube, the static port(s), or both. Moisture
(including ice), dirt, or even insects can cause a blockage in
both systems. During preflight, it is very important to make
sure the pitot tube cover is removed and that static port
openings are checked for blockage and damage.
