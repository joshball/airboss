# Time T = D/GS

Time T = D/GS
To find the time (T) in flight, divide the distance (D) by the
GS. The time to fly 210 NM at a GS of 140 knots is 210 ÷
140 or 1.5 hours. (The 0.5 hour multiplied by 60 minutes
equals 30 minutes.) Answer: 1:30.
