Figure B-5. 3P “Perceive” analysis and PAVE checklist risk identification.
Additional Risk Assessment Tools
The CARE checklist provides a breakdown of rationale pilots use during the 3P “process” stage [Figure B-6]. It includes a
perspective of what could happen (consequences), what may be done to prevent an unwanted outcome (alternatives), a check
of the actual conditions (reality), and an analysis of pilot motivation (external pressures).
Figure B-6. Process using the CARE checklist for risk assessment.
