# Pre-flight IMSAFE

## What it does

A 90-second guided IMSAFE check with honest follow-up questions. Not "are you sick? yes/no" but "how many hours did you sleep? when did you last eat? how's your stress level today?" -- questions that make you actually think.

## Core features

- Guided walk-through of all 6 IMSAFE categories with calibrated follow-up questions
- Adaptive depth: short follow-ups when green, deeper probing when yellow
- Trends over time: "You've reported poor sleep 3 of your last 5 flights"
- Optional journal note attachment for honest self-reflection
- No-go recommendation with reasoning when multiple factors are yellow

## Notes

The biggest challenge is getting pilots to answer honestly instead of clicking through. The UX must feel like a conversation with a trusted friend, not a compliance form. Trend tracking is where the real value lives.
