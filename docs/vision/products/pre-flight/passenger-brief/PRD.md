# Passenger Brief Script

## What it does

Generates a passenger briefing script tailored to who's flying with you (first-time, nervous, experienced), what aircraft, and what route. Covers the legally required items but wraps them in language that actually puts people at ease.

## Core features

- Templates for first-time, nervous, child, and experienced passengers
- Aircraft-specific: door handles, seat belts, emergency exits for your type
- Route-aware: "We'll be over mountains, it may get bumpy" vs. "Short hop over flat terrain"
- Printable card option for passengers to keep during flight
- Covers all legally required briefing items (14 CFR 91.107, 91.519)

## Notes

Simple content generation product. The value is reducing the awkwardness of passenger briefings and ensuring nothing gets skipped. Low priority but easy to build and useful for GA pilots who fly friends and family.
