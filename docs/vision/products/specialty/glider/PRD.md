# Glider Mode

## What it does

Decision reps and knowledge practice for glider pilots. Thermalling decisions, off-field landing selection, tow and winch launch emergencies, and soaring competition strategy.

## Core features

- Thermal decision scenarios: when to leave, when to stay, when to run for the airport
- Off-field landing selection drills with terrain analysis
- Launch emergency procedures for tow and winch
- Weather reading for soaring: cloud streets, convergence, wave
- Competition task planning and speed-to-fly decisions

## Notes

Medium complexity -- glider operations have unique decision patterns but a smaller content library is needed. The glider community is small but passionate and underserved by training technology. Could be a strong niche differentiator.
