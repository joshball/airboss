# Aviation-Curious

## What it does

Accessible content for people who are curious about flying but aren't pilots. What happens in a cockpit? Why do planes do that thing? How do pilots make decisions? A free entry point that demystifies aviation.

## Core features

- "What would you do?" simplified decision scenarios with no jargon
- Explainer content: how weather affects flights, what ATC does, how navigation works
- "Is flying for me?" assessment with realistic expectations
- Links to local flight schools and discovery flight resources
- Free tier -- serves as top-of-funnel for the platform

## Notes

Low complexity and low personal need, but strategically important as a free acquisition funnel. Content must be genuinely interesting to non-pilots, not dumbed-down pilot training. Could drive significant traffic through social sharing of "What would you do?" scenarios.
