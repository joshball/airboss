# Sim Community

## What it does

The same decision scenarios and knowledge products repackaged for flight sim enthusiasts (MSFS, X-Plane, DCS). Not real pilots but a huge audience that cares about realism and wants to learn real procedures.

## Core features

- Scenario library accessible without pilot credentials
- Sim-specific framing: references to sim platforms, keybindings, add-ons
- Community features tailored to virtual aviators
- Bridge content: "Here's how this works in the real airplane"
- Funnel to real training for sim pilots considering a PPL

## Notes

Low complexity -- it's primarily a repackaging of existing content with different marketing. The sim community is 10-100x larger than the real pilot community. Risk is brand dilution if real pilots see it as "not serious." Keep the branding separate.
