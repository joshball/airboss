# Passenger-Pilots

## What it does

Content for frequent airline passengers who want to understand what's happening up front. Not training -- enrichment. Why did we circle? What was that bump? Why are we going around? Turns anxiety into curiosity.

## Core features

- "What just happened?" explanations for common passenger experiences (go-arounds, turbulence, delays)
- Cockpit perspective videos and walkthroughs
- Simplified weather and ATC explainers from the passenger seat
- "Nervous flyer" mode with reassurance-focused content
- Social sharing: "I learned why planes circle before landing"

## Notes

Low complexity, low personal need, but could be a high-traffic content play. Nervous flyer content is a large market. Must be careful not to oversimplify or create false confidence. Different tone than the rest of the platform -- informational, not training.
