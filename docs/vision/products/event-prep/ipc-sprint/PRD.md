# IPC Sprint

## What it does

Same shape as BFR Sprint but focused on instrument proficiency check prep. Approach procedures, holds, intercepting courses, partial panel, weather decision-making. For pilots who lost instrument currency and need to get sharp before the IPC.

## Core features

- 4-week structured program covering IPC ground and procedure knowledge
- Approach plate reading drills, hold entry practice, and course intercept scenarios
- Weather decision scenarios specific to instrument flying
- Partial panel and unusual attitude mental rehearsal
- Readiness assessment aligned with 61.57(d) requirements

## Notes

Depends on Approach Rehearsal (prd:pre:approach-rehearsal) for plate-based practice. The audience is instrument-rated pilots who've gone 6+ months without shooting approaches -- a very common situation. Higher complexity than BFR Sprint because instrument knowledge is deeper and more procedural.
