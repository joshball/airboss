# Interview Prep

## What it does

Structured mock interview practice for airline, 135, and corporate pilot positions. Covers HMAT (Hiring Manager Assessment Tool), technical questions, scenario-based judgment calls, and behavioral interview patterns.

## Core features

- Interview format guides for major airlines, regionals, 135, and corporate
- HMAT-style scenario practice with structured response frameworks
- Technical question bank covering systems, regulations, and weather
- Behavioral interview practice with STAR-format coaching
- Mock interview sessions with scoring and feedback

## Notes

Low personal need since this targets career-track pilots, but it's a clear monetization opportunity. Content requires research into current interview formats at specific operators -- these change frequently. Could partner with interview prep services like Cage Marshall.
