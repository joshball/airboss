# Written Test Prep

## What it does

PPL, IR, and CPL knowledge test prep that teaches understanding through scenarios instead of pure rote memorization. Not "memorize the answer to question #4237" like Sporty's and King -- actually learn the material.

## Core features

- Question banks organized by knowledge area with scenario context
- "Why is this the answer?" explanations that teach the underlying concept
- Adaptive difficulty that focuses time on weak areas
- Practice tests that mirror FAA test format and timing
- Progress tracking with predicted test score

## Notes

Competing with established players (Sporty's, King, Sheppard Air) but differentiated by scenario-anchored learning instead of rote memorization. The bet is that pilots who understand the material outperform pilots who memorize answers -- and retain the knowledge longer.
