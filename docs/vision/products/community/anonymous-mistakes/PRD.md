# Anonymous Mistakes

## What it does

Pilots share mistakes anonymously. Community votes on usefulness. The best submissions become learning content. Like NASA ASRS but social, immediate, and designed to be read -- not filed and forgotten.

## Core features

- Anonymous submission: what happened, what you learned, what you'd do differently
- Community voting on usefulness (not judgment)
- Tagged by mistake type: procedural, decision, communication, automation, weather
- Top submissions promoted to featured learning content
- No identifying details enforced by submission guidelines

## Notes

Low complexity -- fundamentally a moderated anonymous posting system. The challenge is culture, not technology: building a community where admitting mistakes is valued, not punished. Moderation matters to keep quality high and prevent identifying details from leaking through.
