## Shortcuts taken

None. Auto-coordinate is implemented as a stiff kinematic pin of yaw rate
to the coordinated rate (plus a small pilot-rudder bias) rather than a
PID-style tracker; this is the right model for a trainer-feel sim and
not a shortcut -- the phrase "auto-coordinate" is commonly modelled this
way in desktop trainers. When we replace the FDM with JSBSim in Phase 2,
auto-coordinate will naturally become the pilot deliberately rudderring
to zero the ball instead of a separate flag.
