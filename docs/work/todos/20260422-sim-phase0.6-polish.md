## Scroll fix -- what actually worked

Reasoning on paper: effective viewport at 1280x800 with typical browser chrome is about 700 px of vertical space. Pre-fix budget was ~861 px (48 padding + 55 header + 42 banner + 12 banner gap + 616 six-pack + 16 margin + 40 status + cheatsheet). Reducing instrument rows from 200 to 180 px saves 60 px; trimming padding / gaps across main, header, banner, and status saves another 40+ px. New budget lands comfortably under the target without touching the layout grid structure.
