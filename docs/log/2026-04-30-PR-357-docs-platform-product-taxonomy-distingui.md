summary: |
  Formalize the distinction between **products** (full user-facing systems), **features** (pieces of products), and **surface apps** (code dirs). The term \"product\" had been overloaded -- routes like \/lens\, \/goals\, \/credentials\ and rep-type names like \"Spaced Memory Items\" were getting called products when they're really features of one ground-school product. The fragmentation distorted roadmap thinking and naming pressure.
