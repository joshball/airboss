3. APPLICABILITY. The focus of the content of this AC is primarily on operations not
covered by Safety Alerts for Operators (SAFO) 06012, Landing Performance Assessments at
Time of Arrival (Turbojets). Any values or examples provided in this AC are estimated values.
Consult manufacturer information for specific guidance applicable to a given airplane model.
However, in the absence of specific landing performance data provided by the airplane’s
manufacturer, the pilot/operator is encouraged to use the most conservative landing performance
information presented in this AC. Further, the operator may consider these concepts as additional
information in conjunction with an operator’s specific operations specifications (OpSpecs) or
management specifications (MSpecs) and the airplane’s pilot’s operating handbook (POH), or
Airplane Flight Manual (AFM), as appropriate.
