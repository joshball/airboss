11.4.3 Frontal Lift. Frontal lift (see Figure 11-6) occurs when the cold, denser air wedges under
       the warm, less dense air, plowing it upward, and/or the warmer air rides up and over the
       colder air in a process called overrunning. Cloud and precipitation will form given
       sufficient lift and moisture content of the warm air.
