      the sun (see Figure 4-6). The Earth’s rotational axis is tilted by 23½° from the
      perpendicular drawn to the plane of the Earth’s orbit about the sun and points the same
      direction in space all year long. The North Pole is tilted most directly toward the sun
      Solstice (~December 22). Thus, in the Northern Hemisphere, the longest day of the year
