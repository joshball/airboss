2.3.2   Commercial Weather Information Providers.
        Commercial weather information providers are a major source of weather products for
        the aviation community. In general, they produce proprietary weather products based on
        NWS information with formatting and layout modifications, but no material changes to
        the weather information itself. This is also referred to as “repackaging.”

        In other cases, commercial providers produce forecasts, analyses, and other proprietary
        weather products which may substantially differ from the information contained in
        NWS-produced products. Operators who desire to use products prepared by a commercial
        weather provider, as opposed to using products that are simply repackaged, may require
