5.2.1   AIRMET Issuance.
        AIRMETs are issued from the three MWOs located at the AWC, AAWU, and
        WFO Honolulu. Their AORs are:

        •   AWC: The CONUS and adjacent coastal waters (see Figure 5-8, AWC AIRMET
            Areas of Responsibility—Continental U.S.).
        •   AAWU: Alaska and adjacent coastal waters (see Figure 5-9, AAWU Flight Advisory
            and Area Forecast (FA) Zones—Alaska).
        •   WFO Honolulu: Hawaii and adjacent waters (see Figure 5-10, WFO Honolulu
            AIRMET Areas of Responsibility—Hawaii).
