5.24.2 Content and Format.
       The content and format of soaring forecasts vary with the NWS WFO providing the
       forecast, based on the needs of their soaring community. It is beyond the scope of this
       advisory circular (AC) to describe all the many variations of soaring forecasts and their
       content. Soaring pilots should consult with the NWS WFO in their soaring area for more
       information.
