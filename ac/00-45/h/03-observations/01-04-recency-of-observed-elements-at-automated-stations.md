3.1.4   Recency of Observed Elements at Automated Stations.
        For those elements that the human observer evaluates using spatial averaging techniques
        (e.g., sky cover and visibility), the automated station substitutes time averaging of sensor
        data. Therefore, in an automated observation, sky condition is an evaluation of sensor
        data gathered during the 30-minute period ending at the actual time of the observation.
        All other elements are based on sensor data that is within 10 minutes or less of the actual
        time of the observation.
