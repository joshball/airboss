2.3.7 The FAA Safety Team (FAASTeam). Because GA PDs comprise the greatest percent
      of all runway incursions, the FAA has increased its outreach efforts through its Flight
      Standards FAASTeam program. The FAASTeam program provides outreach to
      GA pilots on topics concerning aviation safety. For example, the FAASTeam addresses
      local runway safety issues by posting hot spot charts, runway safety bulletins, runway
      safety event notices, and other materials on its website, https://www.faasafety.gov/.
      Additionally, the FAASTeam also provides web-based training. Pilots and flight
      instructors should become familiar with all FAASTeam resources to learn more about
      runway incursions.
