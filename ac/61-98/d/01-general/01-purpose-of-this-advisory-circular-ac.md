1.1 Purpose of This Advisory Circular (AC). This AC provides information to assist
     General Aviation (GA) pilots, flight instructors, and ground instructors in complying with
     the flight reviews required by Title 14 of the Code of Federal Regulations (14 CFR)
     part 61, § 61.56 and the instrument proficiency check (IPC)/recent flight experience
     requirements of § 61.57. Additionally, this AC provides critical information to enhance
     GA safety.
