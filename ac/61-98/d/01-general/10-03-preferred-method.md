1.10.3 Preferred Method. The preferred method for submitting an Airman Certificate and/or
       Rating Application is through the Integrated Airman Certification and/or Rating
       Application (IACRA) system. IACRA is the web-based certification/rating application
       that guides the user through the FAA’s airman application process. IACRA validates
       data. It also uses electronic signatures to protect the information’s integrity and
       eliminates paper forms.

        •   You can find IACRA at https://iacra.faa.gov/IACRA.
        •   You can find FAA Form 8710-1 at http://www.faa.gov/forms/.
