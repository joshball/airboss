1.8.1 FAA Regulatory English Language Eligibility Requirements. AELS directly affects flight
      safety. Regulations require pilots to meet and maintain English language skill
      requirements for all grades of pilot certificates issued under part 61. When a flight
      instructor conducts a flight evaluation and/or flight training event, the flight instructor
      should identify any pilot who does not meet English language skill requirements and take
      appropriate action. Finally, all training, checking, or evaluation will be conducted in
      English. AC 60-28 provides information and guidance regarding English language
      standards required by part 61.
