1.7.3 Reducing GA Accidents. The FAA added important General Aviation Joint Steering
      Committee (GAJSC) findings and recommendations to AC 61-98 pertaining
      to GA accidents (see paragraph 2.1).

       1. GA pilots should become aware of this information and apply it to their
          personal currency program plans of action.
       2. Flight instructors should apply this information to their training and evaluation
          plans of action.
