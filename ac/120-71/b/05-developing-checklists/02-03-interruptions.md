5.2.3   Interruptions. Crewmembers frequently cannot complete a checklist because of
        interruption or an item on the checklist has not yet been accomplished. Operating
        procedures must be established to ensure that the correct checklist sequence is completed
        after an interruption. If the sequence cannot be re-established or the crew is unclear on
        where to resume the checklist, the appropriate section of the checklist should be
        reaccomplished from the beginning.
