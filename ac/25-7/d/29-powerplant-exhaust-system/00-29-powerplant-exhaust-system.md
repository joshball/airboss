CHAPTER 29. POWERPLANT: EXHAUST SYSTEM


29.1       General—§ 25.1121.

29.1.1     Explanation: Section 25.1121(a)—Carbon Monoxide Contamination.
           Carbon monoxide detection tests are conducted in accordance with this requirement to
           determine that the disposal of exhaust gases from each exhaust system does not cause
           carbon monoxide contamination of any personnel compartment.

29.1.2     References.
           Also see information related to the evacuation of other personnel compartment
           atmosphere contaminants contained in paragraphs 19.1 and 31.2 of this AC addressing
           the requirements of §§ 25.831 and 25.1197, respectively.
