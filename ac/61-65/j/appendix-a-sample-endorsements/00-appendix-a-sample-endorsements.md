                                  SAMPLE ENDORSEMENTS

The following examples are recommended sample endorsements for use by authorized
instructors when endorsing logbooks, or other means found acceptable to the Administrator for
airmen applying for a knowledge or practical test, or when certifying accomplishment of
requirements for pilot operating privileges. Each endorsement must be legible and include the
instructor’s signature, date of signature, certificated flight instructor (CFI) or certificated ground
instructor certificate number, and flight instructor recent experience (RE) end date or certificate
expiration date, as applicable. Signature lines are no longer shown for a sample endorsement
except for endorsements that someone other than a flight instructor or a ground instructor would
make.

For a flight instructor certificate without an expiration date:

                      [date] /s/ J. J. Jones 987654321CFI RE 12-31-2026

For a flight instructor certificate with an expiration date:

                      [date] /s/ J. J. Jones 987654321CFI Exp. 01-31-2025

For a ground instructor:

                      [date] /s/ J. J. Jones 987654321
