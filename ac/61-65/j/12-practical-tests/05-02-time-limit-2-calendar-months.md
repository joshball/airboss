12.5.2 Time Limit 2 Calendar Months. Section 61.39(f) states that all increments, ground/oral
       and flight, of practical tests must be completed within 2 calendar months. The increment
       may be because the test was discontinued for reasons stated in § 61.43(e) or was planned
       to be conducted in increments. This is a separate time limit from the 60-calendar-day
       limit prescribed above that applies to credit for tasks passed.
