1 PURPOSE OF THIS ADVISORY CIRCULAR (AC). This AC provides guidance for
     pilot and instructor applicants, pilots, flight instructors, ground instructors, and examiners
     on certification standards, knowledge test procedures, and other requirements in Title 14
     of the Code of Federal Regulations (14 CFR) part 61. The contents of this document do
     not have the force and effect of law and are not meant to bind the public in any way, and
     the document is intended only to provide information to the public regarding existing
     requirements under the law or agency policies.
