20.4 Solo at Night. A student pilot may not operate an aircraft in solo flight at night unless
     that student pilot has received the training required by § 61.87(o)(1) and (2) at night and
     the endorsement required by § 61.87(o)(3). That endorsement in the student’s logbook is
     for the specific M/M aircraft to be flown for night solo flight. An authorized instructor
     who gave the training within the 90-calendar-day period preceding the date of the flight
     must make the endorsement. A student pilot seeking a sport pilot certificate is not
     authorized to operate an LSA at night per § 61.89(c).
