20.5 Cross-Country. A student pilot who seeks solo cross-country flight privileges must have
     received flight training on the maneuvers and procedures required by § 61.93(e)–(m), as
     appropriate to the M/M of aircraft for which the solo cross-country privileges are sought.
     The student pilot must have demonstrated cross-country proficiency on the appropriate
     maneuvers and procedures of § 61.93 to an authorized instructor. The student pilot must
     have satisfactorily accomplished the pre-solo flight maneuvers and procedures required
     by § 61.87 in the M/M of aircraft or similar M/M of aircraft for which solo cross-country
     privileges are sought, and the student must comply with any limitations included in the
     authorized instructor’s endorsement. A student pilot must have the endorsements
     prescribed in § 61.93(c) for each cross-country flight.
