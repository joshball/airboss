The FAA recommends owners, operators and pilots of the affected aircraft
listed in this InFO conduct a visual inspection of the breakaway pin per SAIB AIR 21-19R1 before
conducting any side pull operations. If the pin is

    •   not secured by the retaining ring, or
    •   not green on one end, or
    •   does not fill the hole through the swivel, or
    •   shows signs of damage,

it should be replaced with pin part number 91106-018 prior to the next side pull operation.
