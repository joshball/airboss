# Week 7 -- Parts 141 and 135

The operation side of "the pilot / the flight / the operation." Cursory treatment. The goal is literacy, not competence. A CFI needs to know what these parts are, when they apply, and where to look -- not how to fly an airline.

## What you'll be able to do by end of week

- Distinguish Part 91 / Part 141 / Part 135 / Part 121 operations and name what differentiates each
- Locate the syllabus for any 141 cert level (Part 141 appendices A-N)
- Locate the operational rules of 135 (subparts D, E, F)
- Recognize when a "Part 91" flight is actually a Part 135 flight in disguise (compensation/hire trap)
