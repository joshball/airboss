# Week 5 -- Part 91 subparts C, D, E

Equipment and maintenance. This is where students get destroyed on checkrides because the inspections are spread across three sections (91.409 / 91.411 / 91.413) and the equipment rules require navigating both 91.205 and 91.213 together.

## What you'll be able to do by end of week

- Recall and apply 91.203 (AROW documents) and 91.7 (PIC airworthiness determination)
- Apply 91.205 to a specific operation (VFR day / VFR night / IFR cumulative lists)
- Walk the no-MEL pathway in 91.213(d) for an inop instrument or piece of equipment
- Recall the inspection currencies: annual (91.409(a)), 100-hour (91.409(b)), altimeter/static (91.411), transponder (91.413), ELT (91.207)
- Apply ADS-B Out requirements (91.225, 91.227)
