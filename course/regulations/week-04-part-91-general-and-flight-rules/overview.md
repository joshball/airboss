# Week 4 -- Part 91 subparts A-B

The flight side of "the pilot / the flight / the operation." This week and the next two are where pilots actually live. 91.3 and 91.13 are the most-cited rules in violations, and subpart B (Flight Rules - General) is where preflight, fuel, alcohol, and weather minimums all live.

## What you'll be able to do by end of week

- Cite and apply 91.3 (PIC authority + emergency deviation) and 91.13 (careless or reckless)
- Walk through 91.103 preflight action and recognize what "all available information" requires
- Distinguish VFR weather minimums in different airspace classes (91.155)
- Apply right-of-way rules (91.111-91.115)
- Apply the IFR-specific sections in subpart B: 91.167 (fuel), 91.169 (alternates and 1-2-3 rule), 91.171 (VOR check), 91.175-91.185 (IFR ops)
- Apply 91.211 (oxygen)
