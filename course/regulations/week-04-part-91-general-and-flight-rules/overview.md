# Week 4 -- Part 91 subparts A-B

The flight side of "the pilot / the flight / the operation." This week and the next two are where pilots actually live. 91.3 and 91.13 are the most-cited rules in violations, and subpart B (Flight Rules - General) is where preflight, fuel, weather minimums, right-of-way, and IFR ops all live.

Part 91 is the part you'll be reading once a week for the rest of your career. Week 4 walks the spine: PIC authority, careless or reckless, airworthiness, preflight, occupant rules, right-of-way, minimum altitudes, VFR weather, IFR ops, oxygen, transponder. The whole "any flight, any day" rulebook.

## What you'll be able to do by end of week

- Cite and apply [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/3?at=2026) (PIC authority + emergency deviation) and [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/13?at=2026) (careless or reckless)
- Walk through [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/103?at=2026) preflight action and recognize what "all available information" requires under the Walker letter
- Distinguish [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/105?at=2026) crew seatbelt rules from [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/107?at=2026) passenger seatbelt rules
- Apply right-of-way under [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/111?at=2026), [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/113?at=2026), [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/115?at=2026) and minimum safe altitudes under [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/119?at=2026)
- State VFR fuel reserves, cloud clearances, special VFR conditions, and VFR cruising altitudes ([@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/151?at=2026), [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/155?at=2026), [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/157?at=2026), [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/159?at=2026))
- Apply the IFR ops sections in subpart B: [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/167?at=2026) (fuel), [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/169?at=2026) (alternates and 1-2-3 rule), [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/175?at=2026) through [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/185?at=2026) (DH/MDA, contact/visual, lost comm)
- Apply [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/211?at=2026) (oxygen) and [@cite](airboss-ref:regs/cfr-14/91/215?at=2026) (transponder)
