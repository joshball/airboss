# Week 8 -- Companion documents

The regulatory ecosystem outside Title 14. Pilots who never read the AIM, ACs, or Chief Counsel letters miss most of the practical interpretation of the regulations.

## What you'll be able to do by end of week

- Navigate the AIM by chapter and subsection
- Find the right AC for a given regulatory question by AC numbering scheme
- Locate authoritative Chief Counsel interpretations
- Distinguish what's regulatory (CFR), guidance (AC), expected (AIM), and interpretive (Chief Counsel)
