# Week 2 -- Part 61, the pilot

The pilot side of "the pilot / the flight / the operation." This week is deep. Heavy front-to-back reading of Part 61 subparts A-G with running commentary on the parallel structure.

## What you'll be able to do by end of week

- Map any pilot, situation, or question into the right Part 61 subpart
- Recall and apply 61.51 (logging), 61.56 (flight review), 61.57 (currency), 61.58 (PIC proficiency check)
- Distinguish currency (legal floor) from recency (your own usage record) from proficiency (actual capability)
- Locate medical certificate requirements (Part 67) and BasicMed (49 USC 44703)
