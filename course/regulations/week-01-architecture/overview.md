# Week 1 -- The architecture of Title 14

This week is the navigation map itself. Skip it and the rest of the course collapses into memorization. Treat it as foundation.

## What you'll be able to do by end of week

- Given any pilot question, point at the Part / subpart / section where the answer lives in <30 seconds
- Read a citation aloud (example: 91.169(c)(1)(i)) and parse it correctly
- Name the main parts that affect pilots and what each is for
- Distinguish between regulatory documents (CFR), guidance (AC), expected knowledge (AIM), and interpretation (Chief Counsel)
- Apply the "the pilot / the flight / the operation" framing to any regulatory question
