# Week 9 -- Enforcement and NTSB Part 830

What happens when things go wrong. The compliance program, certificate action, post-incident reporting, and the career-enders.

## What you'll be able to do by end of week

- Distinguish FAA compliance program (formerly compliance philosophy) from legal enforcement
- Walk the enforcement progression: warning notice -> letter of investigation -> certificate action
- Know your rights: Pilot's Bill of Rights (PBR2), NASA ASRS protection
- Cite and apply the career-enders: 61.16, 91.17, 67.403
- Apply NTSB Part 830 (49 CFR 830) -- accident vs. incident, what's reportable when
