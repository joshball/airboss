# Week 3 -- Part 61 subpart H, the CFI

The CFI side of Part 61. You're becoming this; the regulation defines what you are authorized to do.

## What you'll be able to do by end of week

- Cite CFI eligibility (61.183), aeronautical experience (61.187), logbook records (61.189)
- Distinguish privileges (61.193) from limitations (61.195) and apply both
- Walk through CFI renewal pathways (61.197): FIRC, practical test, recent activity record
- Find any endorsement in AC 61-65 by category and apply it correctly
