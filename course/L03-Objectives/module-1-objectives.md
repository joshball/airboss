# Module 1 Learning Objectives

Instructor Foundations & Modern Cockpit. Derived from L02 knowledge research (A.1, A.8, A.12) and the competency graph.

**Competencies targeted:** AV-1, AV-2, AV-3, RC-4, ES-3

## Guiding Question

> You're a CFI in 2026. The cockpit changed, the standards changed, and they'll keep changing. Can you teach safely in that world?

---

## A.1 -- GPS, Automation, TAA

Source: [L02 A.1 research](../L02-Knowledge/A.1_GPS-Automation-TAA/)

### Domain Knowledge Objectives

| ID | Objective | Competency | Assessment |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| M1-01 | Identify the three categories of automation hazard (dependency, fixation, mode confusion) and explain how each leads to different accident chains | AV-1 | Scenario recognition, knowledge check |
| M1-02 | Explain GPS database currency requirements and the operational consequences of using expired navigation data | AV-3 | Scenario (SCN 1.1), knowledge check |
| M1-03 | Describe what qualifies an aircraft as TAA under 14 CFR SS 61.1 and SS 61.129(j), including the four installed-component criteria | AV-3 | Knowledge check |
| M1-04 | Distinguish ADS-B Out (regulatory requirement) from ADS-B In (optional), and explain FIS-B weather latency (15-20 min) as a tactical limitation | AV-3 | Knowledge check, scenario (SCN 1.5) |
| M1-05 | Explain RAIM requirements for GPS approaches and what a pilot must do when RAIM is unavailable | AV-3 | Scenario (SCN 1.3) |
| M1-06 | Describe NextGen's impact on GA at an overview level -- PBN, ADS-B surveillance, changing approach infrastructure | AV-3 | Knowledge check |

| ID | Objective | Competency | Assessment |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| M1-07 | Recognize automation dependency in a student (over-reliance on moving map, failure to cross-check, no backup plan) and respond with the least invasive effective intervention | AV-1 | Scenario (SCN 1.4, 1.5) |
