Task B. Recovery from Unusual Flight Attitudes
References: FAA-H-8083-2, FAA-H-8083-3, FAA-H-8083-15, FAA-H-8083-25; POH/AFM
Objective: To determine the applicant exhibits satisfactory knowledge, risk management, and skills associated with
recovering from unusual flight attitudes solely by reference to instruments.
Knowledge:      The applicant demonstrates understanding of:
IR.IV.B.K1      Procedures for recovery from unusual attitudes in flight.
IR.IV.B.K2      Prevention of unusual attitudes, including flight causal, physiological, and environmental factors, and
system and equipment failures.
IR.IV.B.K3      Procedures available to safely regain visual meteorological conditions (VMC) after flight into
inadvertent instrument meteorological conditions or unintended instrument meteorological conditions
(IIMC)/(UIMC).
IR.IV.B.K4      Appropriate use of automation, if applicable.
