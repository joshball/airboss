# Calibration

**Confidence calibration.** How well your "I know this" matches your actual hit rate.

When you grade a card "I had this," calibration measures whether you really did. A well-calibrated learner is right when they say they're right, and right about being wrong. Calibration is one of the strongest predictors of pilot decision-making under pressure.
