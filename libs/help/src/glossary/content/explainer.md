# Explainer

The collapsible "Why am I here?" block at the top of a page. Two or three sentences: what is this page, who is it for, what should you do.

Open by default. The user can dismiss per-page (the dismissal sticks) or hide them all globally from Settings. Even when dismissed, a small `?` button stays visible to re-open.
