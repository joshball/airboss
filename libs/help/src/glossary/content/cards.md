# Cards

**Memory review** -- spaced repetition. Short-form recall of a single fact or step.

Cards come back to you on an interval that grows when you remember and shrinks when you don't. Each card is attached to a knowledge node so the right thing comes up at the right time.

Cards are the substrate for memorisation. **Reps** test decision-making on top of memorised facts.
