# Knowledge node

One atomic teachable concept in the airboss knowledge graph. Examples: "VFR weather minimums in Class E above 10,000 ft," "Define angle of attack," "Compute true airspeed from indicated airspeed."

Each node links to syllabus tasks, FAA references, and review cards. The knowledge graph is what holds everything together: cards measure recall on a node, reps measure decision-making over a cluster of nodes, and the lens views surface the nodes that need work.

Nodes follow **discovery-first pedagogy** -- lead with WHY; let the learner derive the answer; reveal regulations as confirmation, not as arbitrary rules.
