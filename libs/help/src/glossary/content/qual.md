# Qual

**Qualifications** -- the certificates you are working toward. Common quals: Private Pilot (PPL), Instrument Rating (IR), Commercial Pilot (CPL), Certificated Flight Instructor (CFI).

The DB calls this a `credential`; the user word is **Quals**. Each qual is backed by one or more FAA syllabi (an ACS or PTS) that lists the areas of operation, tasks, and elements you are responsible for.

Quals are the long-lived target. A goal slices off a chunk of one qual (or several) and makes it your current focus.
